This is an online examination where the candidates must solve the question paper in one 1 hour for 100 marks. The test consists of 3 sections. Candidates must know that the sectional cut-off in the Preliminary Exam has been removed and the overall score of all the sections will be considered for selection to the next round.. The cut-off or minimum passing marks for each section is decided by the Bank each year depending on the difficult of the test.

The SBI PO Mains pattern comprises of 155 questions which candidates need to answer within a duration of 3 hours. The exam has been segregated into two different types of tests –

  1. Objective
  2. Descriptive

The timing and exam pattern of both the tests are different from one another. The Objective Test is an online test with separate timing for each section and the Descriptive test has a duration of 30 minutes comprising of two questions (50 marks each) which tests the candidates’ written English language skills like a letter or essay writing.
