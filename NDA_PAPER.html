The Mathematics paper contains 300 marks while the General Ability test holds 600 marks. The written test will be of a total of 900 marks.For every incorrect response, 0.33 marks will be deducted as negative marking in the examination. After clearing the written test, candidates have to go through the SSB Interview. The interview process will have a total of 900 marks. The question paper of Mathematics and PART B of the General Ability test will be available in both English and Hindi languages.Paper 1 in NDA is purely based on Mathematics. No change is observed in NDA Syllabus 2020 from the previous year. The questions will be asked from 10+2 level only. The sub-sections in this part are also of basic-level mathematics.
